Valuation Methodology & Calculation Glossary

1. Gross Revenue (Monthly) Formula:
Gross Revenue = Daily Foot Traffic × (Traffic Conversion Rate / 100) × Average Item Price × Items Per Order × 30 Days

2. Net Profit (Monthly) Formula:
Net Profit = (Gross Revenue × Profit Margin / 100) − Platform Fee − Transaction Fee − Cellular Data Fee − POS Device Rental Fee − AI Recognition Fee

3. Payback Period Formula:
Payback Period = (Product Price × Device Units) / Net Profit
